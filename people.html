Discover the Diverse Charms of Batangas





The people here are different than other Filipinos. They are called Batangueños, people who are well known for having bright personalities. They have perfect smiles, and love to deliver humorous lines during conversations. .

Other than this, they are one of the best people to bond with if you would like to drink beers. Their conversations and way of drinking is one of the bests if you are trying to experience the Filipino way of drinking

Give Batangas a visit and you'll never forget it.
