Central Azucarera Don Pedro Inc.





Aside from rentals beside the beach as a source of money, most of the people here work in a sugar manufacturing factory also known as Central Azucarera Don Pedro Inc. or Azucarera.

Estimated, about 13,000 tons of sugar cane are delivered daily to the factory by the use of trucks.

People here love working in Azucarera because they provide great wages, bonuses and take care of their employees big time.
