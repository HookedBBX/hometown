TOP 5 TOURIST ATTRACTIONS



First on the list, Fortune Island in Nasugbu, Batangas, is a special place with a mix of history and beauty. You can get there by boat. The island looks like ancient Greece with old columns and statues.

It's like stepping into a picture! You can enjoy the blue sea and clean beaches at Fortune Island Resort. If you like adventure, there's even a place to dive from cliffs!

There's also a shipwreck to explore. It's a simple yet cool spot to relax and have fun by the sea.

St. Francis Xavier Church in Nasugbu, Batangas, is a beautiful church with a rich history. Its peaceful surroundings and traditional architecture make it a serene place for prayer and reflection.

The church is dedicated to St. Francis Xavier, and visitors appreciate its quiet charm and spiritual atmosphere.

Whether you're a local resident or a visitor exploring Nasugbu, a visit to St. Francis Xavier Church offers a glimpse into the town's religious and cultural heritage.




Canyon Cove in Nasugbu, Batangas, is a fantastic beach getaway with a laid-back vibe. The resort offers a beautiful stretch of sandy shores along with clear blue waters.

It's a perfect place to unwind and enjoy the sun, sand, and sea. The ambiance is relaxed, making it great for families or those seeking a peaceful retreat.

With comfortable accommodations and picturesque views, Canyon Cove is a simple yet delightful destination for those looking to escape and relax by the beach in Nasugbu.




Matabungkay Beach in Nasugbu, Batangas, is a wonderful spot for beach lovers like you!

The soft sandy shores and clear waters create a perfect setting for relaxation and fun. Whether you're into swimming, building sandcastles, or just soaking up the sun, Matabungkay Beach has it all.

The beach is surrounded by coconut trees, giving it a tropical feel. It's an ideal place for a day of leisure or a weekend getaway, offering a simple yet enjoyable beach experience in Nasugbu.
